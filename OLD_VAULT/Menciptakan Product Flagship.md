
- A product roadmap is a visual plan that maps out the vision and direction of your product offering over time. It shows the what and why of the product to help you choose the right development direction at each stage of full cycle product development. Product roadmaps act as a shared source of truth for the product’s overall direction, priorities, dependencies, and progress over time. They’re a guiding strategic document and a plan for executing your product strategy.
    
- Participants will learn about the benefits of creating a product roadmap for their startups, where a good product roadmap can help the startup to:
    
	o define a clear product vision or strategy statement, o monitor development progress,

	o help them prioritize work and choose the most valuable product features,

	o help manage resources efficiently.

-  Participants will also learn about different types of product roadmap and how to build a product roadmap using different tools and frameworks.
    
-  Mentors will share a step-by-step on how to build a product roadmap, as well
    as how to set goals and strategies for their product roadmap.
